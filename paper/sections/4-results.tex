\subsection{Metrics}

Jaccard similarity index (A and B are sets):

\begin{equation}
  J(A, B) = \frac{|A \cup B|}{|A \cap B|}
\end{equation}

can be calculated as:

\begin{equation}
  J(A, B) = \frac{TP}{TP + FN + FP}
\end{equation}

Threshold Jaccard Index:

\begin{equation}
  TJ(A, B) = \begin{cases}
      J(A, B), & \text{if}\ J(A, B) \geq{0.65} \\
      0, & \text{otherwise}
    \end{cases}
\end{equation}

Dice:

\begin{equation}
  Dice = \frac{2 * TP}{2 * TP + FN + FP}
\end{equation}

F1-Score:

\begin{equation}
  Dice = \frac{TP}{2 * TP + FN + FP}
\end{equation}

Sensitivity:

\begin{equation}
  Sensitivity = \frac{TP}{TP + FN}
\end{equation}

Specificity:

\begin{equation}
  Sensitivity = \frac{TN}{TN + FP}
\end{equation}

Accuracy:

\begin{equation}
  Accuracy = \frac{TP + TN}{TP + FP + TN + FN}
\end{equation}
